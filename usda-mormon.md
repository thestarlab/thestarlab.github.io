# USDA: Modeling Spatiotemporal Distribution of Mormon Crickets with Geospatial Artificial Intelligence

The goal of this project was to develop GeoAI methods to forecast Mormon cricket populations and potential outbreaks. This project aims to achieve two objectives: 1) develop a GeoAI based, uncertainty aware method for spatiotemporal distribution and outbreak forecasting of Mormon crickets; and 2) understand the effects of environmental, climate conditions and pest management practices on the spatiotemporal distribution of Mormon crickets.

## Software

- [Bayesian_SDM_MC](https://www.spatstat.com/Bayesian_SDM_MC/MC.html): An online mapping application for spatiotemporal distribution modeling and outbreak forecasting of Mormon crickets.
